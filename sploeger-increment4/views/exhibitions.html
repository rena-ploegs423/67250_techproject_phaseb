Exhibition 1: Reflections in Motion

This exhibition examines how light, sound, and movement can shape the way visitors experience a space. Through immersive installations and interactive digital works, Reflections in Motion invites guests to consider how technology can transform ordinary environments into artistic experiences.

Exhibition 2: Future Memory

Future Memory explores the relationship between personal memory and digital archives. Featuring multimedia pieces, projection art, and interactive storytelling, the exhibition asks how technology preserves, reshapes, and reimagines the stories we carry with us.
